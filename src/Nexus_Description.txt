[center][size=6]Dwarven Expansion Bundles[/size][/center]
Adjusts the Community Center Bundles to include various cave focused items and items from Dwarven Expansion.

As Bundles are set at game start, this mod does nothing on an existing game and is not compatible with other mods that alter the same bundles.

[size=5]Bundle Adjustments[/size]

[size=4]Spring Crops[/size]
Adds Cave Carrot as a crop that can be donated.

[size=4]Summer Crops[/size]
Adds Void Berry as a crop that can be donated.

[size=4]Fall Crops[/size]
Adds Midnight Gourd as a crop that can be donated.

[size=4]Quality Crops[/size]
Adds 5 Gold-star Midnight Gours as a crop that can be donated.

[size=4]Animal[/size]
Adds Copper Crab Egg, Iron Crab Egg, Gold Crab Egg and Iridium Crab Egg as options to donate.

[size=4]Artisan[/size]
Adds Infused Dwarven Spirits and Gold Emerald Ring as options for donation.

[size=4]Specialty Fish[/size]
Adds Lava Eel as a donation option.

[size=4]Chef's[/size]
Adds Ice Pip Soup as a donation option.

[size=4]Enchanter's[/size]
Adds Prismatic Dust as a donation option.
